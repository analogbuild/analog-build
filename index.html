The Concept

Building a home shouldn't require a permanent commitment to a place, a price, or a plan that no longer fits.


analog.build is a design and logistics platform built around one idea — that the structure should follow the person, not the other way around.


Configure your space. Know your cost. Order the build. When life changes — and it will — disassemble, relocate, and reconfigure. The only cost is logistics.


Not a compromise. A feature.

Principles
01

Liquid by Design

A structure that can be moved is an asset, not a liability. Decisions should cost logistics — not years of your life.

02

Buy Only What You Need

Start with one container. Add a module when you need it. Never carry more than your current life requires.

03

Dignity in Simplicity

A 160 sq ft space, designed well and built clean, can be more honest than 4,000 sq ft of compromise.

04

The Supply Chain is the Product

Design, cost, source, deliver, assemble. One system. No brokers. No middlemen. No surprises.

05

Off Grid is On Purpose

Reduced grid dependence isn't a constraint — it's the design. Lower usage. Higher efficiency. More freedom.

06

Architecture Without Apology

The aesthetic doesn't have to suffer for the budget. Fresh. Modern. Anything-goes. Just honest about what it is.

Process

From idea to address
in four steps.

01

Design

Configure your layout in 3D. Stack, bury, rotate. See exactly what you're building before anything is ordered.

02

Cost

Every decision has a number attached. Containers, finishes, fixtures, shipping — live and transparent.

03

Deliver

Flatbed. Rail. Ship. Self-driving delivery. The structure comes to you — wherever you've decided that is.

04

Life changes. Add a module. Move a module. Sell the land. Take the building with you. Start again.

Vision

For people starting out.
And starting over.

"Creating new space from nothing should be more like ordering an Uber than building a whole home."

This system was built for people who need a room, a pool, a reset, a workplace, a guest space — without the permanence, debt, or paralysis that currently comes attached to all of it.

It was built for the person who shouldn't be trying to afford what they think they should have. For the investor who understands that real estate works — and wants to know what it looks like when it also moves.

For the architect who wants to show a client something that didn't exist last week. For the family that needs one more room and doesn't want a renovation.

The structure is the easy part. analog.build is the system around it.
